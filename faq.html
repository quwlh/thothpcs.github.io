About us

What do we do?

We do all types of things which you can find in the drop down menu in the place an order page. We mainly build PC's but we also do repairs, sell prebuilts and a ton of other things. Anything that you can see on that dropdown menu is what we do.

How does it work?

When you fill out the form in place an order, we get an email and then can start contacting you directly about what we can do for you and what service you would like. We consistently check to see if any new orders have come through and by doing it like this, it opens us to a more personal line of communication allowing us to build your trust and help you feel confident using our services.

How does payment work?

Payment and other matters related to that will be discussed during emails and when we recieve your order. They vary based on a lot of different factors and what the customer is asking us to do.
